About Me

Dr. Ahmed’s career journey reflects continuous success, starting as a junior administrator and rising to COO of a healthcare organization. He holds a BA in Arts from Ain Shams University, a Master’s in Management from the Arab Academy, and a Doctorate in Business Administration with a focus on administration management.

With extensive experience in reputable firms and international companies, including Saudi German Hospitals, Dr. Ahmed specializes in business improvement, performance planning, and organizational development. As a freelance consultant and trainer, he has supported hospitals and healthcare entities such as Al Razi Medical Center, Elnokhba Center, and Rayhana Hospital in enhancing culture, leadership, marketing, and strategic planning.

He delivers training on leadership, strategic management, change management, KPIs, BSC, and MBA/DBA modules, with a passion for unlocking organizational potential and driving sustainable growth.

Medical

As the founder of Medics Connect, my vision was to pioneer a breakthrough in healthcare by introducing Teleradiology services to Yemen, the Arab world, and the Horn of Africa.

From the start, the company has positioned itself as a leader, providing 24/7/365 coverage for hospitals and diagnostic centers. Beyond delivering advanced technology, we offer comprehensive solutions that cover the full scope of radiology business requirements in the most efficient and cost-effective way.

Our services include expert consultations and reporting across multiple imaging modalities, such as:

  • • Computed & Digital Radiography (CR/DR)
  • • CT Scans
  • • MRI Scans
  • • Nuclear Medicine Scans
  • • Mammography

Through our secure digital platform, we enable the safe transmission of medical images between locations, allowing highly qualified radiologists to provide accurate interpretations and reports that directly support better patient outcomes.

For me, this company is not just a business—it is a **personal mission** to bridge medical gaps, connect patients with world-class expertise, and drive a real revolution in medical technology across the region.

Through these services, Bashuaib Medical Services is driving positive transformation in healthcare**, empowering institutions and improving patient outcomes across the region.

Operational Excellence
